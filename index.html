About ICCFR

Together We Can Build A Better Future For Every Child

International Children Care Fund Raising (ICCFR) is a nonprofit initiative committed to improving the lives of vulnerable children through education, healthcare, nutrition, and community support.

Our mission is to empower children with opportunities that allow them to grow in safe, healthy, and nurturing environments.
